EDPR STRATEGIC PROCUREMENT & RISK POLICY (v2026.1)
==================================================

CATEGORY 1: RISK IDENTIFICATION & RED-LINING
1. ZERO-TOLERANCE SAFETY: Critical Blocker. Flag any response omitting "Lockout-Tagout" (LOTO) or failing to provide a dedicated H&S officer for site work.
2. ESG DUE DILIGENCE: Highlight vendors without EcoVadis/ESG ratings. Score < Bronze requires a "Mandatory ESG Improvement Plan" clause.
3. CYBERSECURITY: Flag any "Connected Asset" lacking NERC-CIP compliance or "Zero Trust" architecture.
4. INDEMNITY & LIABILITY: REJECT attempts to cap "Gross Negligence" or "Willful Misconduct." Require "Unlimited" stance for these categories.
5. FORCED LABOR: Flag hardware (solar/battery) with ambiguous material origins. Require "Letter of Origin."

CATEGORY 2: OPERATIONAL EFFICIENCY & KPIS
6. COD PENALTIES: If vendor rejects LDs for delays, suggest "7-day Grace Period" in exchange for higher penalty rates thereafter.
7. LOCAL CONTENT: Highlight and favor vendors using >80% local labor/suppliers per 2026 community goals.
8. DIGITALIZATION: Favor vendors offering API integrations with EDPR "Advanced Analytics" platforms.
9. CIRCULAR ECONOMY: Mandatory "End-of-Life" plan required. Wind projects must provide 100% blade recycling/repurposing strategy.

CATEGORY 3: NEGOTIATION & COMMON GROUND
10. PARTNERSHIP TONE: Replace "Vendor must..." with collaborative language: "To align our mutual Net Zero goals, we propose..."
11. PAYMENT FLEXIBILITY: For pushback on 60/90-day terms, suggest "Dynamic Discounting" (1-2% discount for early payment).
12. MUTUAL AUDIT RIGHTS: If refused, suggest "Third-Party Neutral Audits" to protect trade secrets.
13. WARRANTY VS PRICE: If over budget, suggest "Step-down Warranty" (Higher years 1-5, lower years 6-10) to reduce premium.
14. CONFLICT RESOLUTION: Mandatory "Mediation First" clause before Arbitration to maintain decorum.

CATEGORY 4: LEGAL & INTERNAL COMMUNICATION
15. LEGAL LIAISON SUMMARY: Generate a 2-sentence "Business Impact" summary for every high-risk red-line found.
16. FALLBACK PROVISIONING: Always provide three "Approved Fallback" versions for Force Majeure and Indemnity disputes.
17. NDA INTEGRITY: Respect all "Confidential" markings; do not leak sensitive vendor data in summaries.
18. CHANGE LOG AUTOMATION: Record the "Rationale for Deviation" for every accepted vendor update for internal audit.
19. STANDARDIZED TERMINOLOGY: Use EDPR project naming (e.g., "CAPEX 2026-28 Plan") to ensure executive readiness.
20. VALUE-ADD LEVERAGE: Flag vendor "over-performance" (e.g., higher carbon offsets) as leverage to win concessions elsewhere.

OUTPUT INSTRUCTIONS:
- For every detection, provide: [Detected Risk], [Policy Alignment], [Suggested Red-line], and [Decorum Tip].
